Stephen Curry

Wardell Stephen Curry II is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors in the NBA. He is a six-time NBA All-Star, he has been named the NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) twice and won three NBA championships with the Warriors. Many players and analysts have called him the greatest shooter in NBA history. He is credited with revolutionizing the game of basketball by inspiring teams to regularly utilize the three-point shot as part of their winning strategy.
